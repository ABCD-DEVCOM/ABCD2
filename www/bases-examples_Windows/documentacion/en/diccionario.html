Dictionary of terms

The dictionary of terms of the requested field is presented. It is built from the Field Selection Table (FST) of the database.

To advance to another term, write the first letters in the corresponding box and click on Continue.

To continue browsing the list starting from the last term presented, click on More Terms.

Select one or more terms from the list. Click on Search to locate the records which correspond to your selection or on Pass the selected terms to move the selected terms to place them in the search form. These terms will pass to the form between quotation marks indicating that they should be used as they appear. If you select more than one term, they will be linked with the boolean operator OR.
